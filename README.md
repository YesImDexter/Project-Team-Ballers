## 💡 What is HeadHuntr?

HeadHuntr throws out the traditional resume entirely and replaces it with something far more honest — **real work, real results, and real connections.**

The platform targets the Southeast Asian talent market, particularly Malaysia, where graduate underemployment is among the highest in the region. Instead of keyword-stuffed CVs and generic job postings, HeadHuntr matches students with employers based on **actual, verifiable work**.

---

## ⚙️ How the System Works

### 1. 📂 Portfolio-First Candidate Profiles
Students upload real proof of their skills — GitHub projects, design portfolios, case study decks, spreadsheets, or video walkthroughs. The platform's AI scans these files, extracts practical skills from the content, and builds a clean candidate profile — without exposing sensitive or confidential details.

> No more inflating bullet points. Your work speaks for itself.

### 2. 🎫 Employer Demand Tickets
Instead of generic job descriptions, employers post a **Demand Ticket** — a specific, real problem their team is trying to solve. Examples:
- *"We need someone who can build a data dashboard from scratch."*
- *"We're looking for a designer who can redesign a confusing onboarding flow."*

This eliminates mismatched applications and ensures candidates see challenges relevant to their actual skill set.

### 3. 🤖 AI-Powered Matching
The AI reads both the employer's Demand Ticket and the candidate's portfolio, then identifies genuine fit — not just keyword overlap. Candidates are surfaced to roles that suit them; employers only see candidates who can actually deliver.

### 4. 💘 Swipe-to-Match Interface
The experience mirrors a modern matching app:
- **Candidates** see the company challenge, expected timeline, and salary data upfront.
- **Employers** see an anonymized skill breakdown tied to specific portfolio evidence (a project, a file, a piece of code).
- When **both sides match**, a private chat room is unlocked for further conversation and a small, relevant trial task — before any final hiring decision.

### 5. 📈 Skill Gap Feedback Loop
If a student's portfolio doesn't yet meet the threshold for a role, HeadHuntr doesn't just reject them — it tells them **exactly what's missing**: a type of project to build, a tool to learn, or a skill gap to close. This makes HeadHuntr a long-term career companion throughout a student's university journey.
